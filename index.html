Freetown, Sierra Leone

Sierra Leone is a small, vibrant country located on the west coast of Africa, bordered by Guinea, Liberia, and the Atlantic Ocean. Its capital, Freetown, has historical significance as a settlement for freed slaves and is home to the iconic Cotton Tree. The country is rich in iron-ore, bauxite, diamonds. cocoa and coffee, etc. It has about 12 tribes. The country is divided into Western, North-West, South and Eastern Regions.
